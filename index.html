This is a generated tactical vignette, not a reconstruction of a named battle. The force packages follow period organizational patterns, then apply the severe under-strength conditions common in 1943–44.

Map scale. Counters are deliberately oversized. Zoom in to replace them with representative low-poly soldiers, guns, armored vehicles, and transport—not literal one-model-per-man formations.
Combat model. Fire, cover, entrenchment, suppression, morale, fatigue, ammunition, weather, and local numerical advantage interact. Casualties are indicative, not a historical prediction.
Casualty display. Personnel figures fade away without gore as effective strength falls. Destroyed vehicles leave dark wrecks; smoke dissipates while the wreck remains.
Omitted. Corps-level artillery, air power, adjacent formations, medical evacuation, and rear services beyond the visible regimental transport columns are abstracted.
